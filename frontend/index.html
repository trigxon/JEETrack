About

Built by a JEE aspirant,
for JEE aspirants

JEETrack was born out of frustration with scattered spreadsheets and notes. We built the tool we wished existed — one place to track everything, stay consistent, and actually know if you're on track to hit your target score.

It's free, it's fast, and it's built specifically for the JEE pattern. No generic study apps. Just JEE.
