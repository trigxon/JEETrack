Everything you need to know about JEETrack and how it helps you crack JEE.

JEETrack is a free web app built for JEE aspirants to track mock test scores, study hours, syllabus progress, backlogs, and get AI-powered weekly analysis — all in one place. No spreadsheets, no chaos.
Yes, JEETrack is 100% free. Create an account and start tracking your entire JEE preparation — mock tests, hours, syllabus, AI insights — at no cost.
Yes. JEETrack has dedicated modules for both JEE Mains and JEE Advanced with separate score tracking, subject-wise accuracy analysis, trend charts, and a side-by-side comparison view.
The AI looks at your mock test scores, study hours, syllabus completion, and backlog data and gives you a brutally honest weekly breakdown — what to fix, what's working, and where to focus. No fluff, just actionable advice.
Yes. Log lecture, practice, and revision hours for Physics, Chemistry, and Maths every day. JEETrack shows a visual heatmap, daily averages, and streaks — so you can see exactly where your time is going.
The Syllabus Tracker covers every chapter of Physics, Chemistry, and Maths for JEE Mains and Advanced. Mark chapters done for theory and practice separately, and see your completion percentage at a glance.
The Backlog module is where skipped chapters, unsolved DPPs, and missed revisions go. Clear them one by one, build your streak, and make sure nothing falls through the cracks as your exam approaches.
Yes. JEETrack is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works on all devices — Android, iPhone, tablet, and desktop. You can install it directly on your phone's home screen for quick access, no app store needed.
JEETrack caches key assets so the app loads quickly even on slow connections. A stable internet connection is needed to sync and save your data to the cloud.
