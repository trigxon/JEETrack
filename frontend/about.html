The Story
Why JEETrack exists

Every JEE aspirant knows the feeling — you've been "studying hard" for weeks, but when the mock results come in, you have no idea why your score didn't move. You can't see patterns. You don't know which subjects are bleeding points. You're flying blind.

JEETrack was built to fix that. One place to track everything — mock tests, study hours, syllabus completion, backlogs, and AI-powered weekly insights that actually tell you what to do next. No spreadsheets. No guesswork.

JEETrack was born out of frustration with scattered spreadsheets and notes. We built the tool we wished existed.
